Classic Spaghetti

Mama mia!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Passata (or canned tomatoes)
  • Celery
  • Carrots
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Chop up celery, carrots, and onion to make soffrito
  2. Heat olive oil in pan and add soffrito. Cook about 10 mins, or until clear.
  3. Add passata or cannned tomatoes.
  4. Add pinch of salt, adjust to taste.
  5. Cook on super low heat for as long as you can, at least an hour.
  6. Boil up water, add spaghetti and cook according to package.
  7. Right before it's done, take a cupful of the boiling spaghetti water.
  8. Drain spaghetti when done, add back to pan.
  9. Toss with sauce and splash of spaghetti water.
