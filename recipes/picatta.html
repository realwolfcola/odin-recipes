Chicken Picatta Recipe

A delightful wombo combo of lightly battered chicken, capers, and lemon butter.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Capers
  • Lemons

Steps

  1. Pound chicken breasts thin
  2. Dredge chicken in flour
  3. Fry those badboys up
  4. Remove from pan when fried up
  5. Add butter to pan, melty melty
  6. When melted, add capers and lemon juice
  7. Pour lemon butter sauce over chicken
  8. Enjoy
