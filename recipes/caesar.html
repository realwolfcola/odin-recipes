Caesar Salad

Caesar Salad Recipe

A classic. Don't think you really need a description of Caesar Salad, unless you're an alien. And you're not an alien.

Ingredients

  • Romaine lettuce
  • Caesar Dressing (yeah I'm cheaping out here)
  • Croutons
  • Parmesean
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Chop up the romaine. Or don't and make it fancy, whatever man.
  2. Throw it in a bowl.
  3. Mix with dressing, croutons, and parm.
  4. Serve with other stuff or snarf down by itself.
